MATH 151 (Calculus 1) is a course required for all engineering students. It is 4 credit hours and consists of a lecture section and a lab/recitation section. The lecture and recitation focus on vectors, derivatives, and functions while the lab consists of solving problems related to the lecture through python coding. There are four common exams, including the final, that make up 80% of the grade (20% each). The rest comes from homework, lab, and recitation. Prerequisites include a C or better in MATH 150 or in an equivalent course.

PLACEMENT RESULTS
During the spring semester of the freshman year, engineering students all participate in the Entry to a Major process. Through a holistic application, students rank their top 5 choices of major both at their current campus or another A&M campus. The application requires students to write three 250 word essays about why they are interested in EACH of their indicated choices. If the student has a cumulative GPA of 3.75 or higher from their first two semesters, they will be given their first choice major. Anyone seeking guidance on the application process of ETAM as a whole can contact their assigned academic advisors. It is crucial that you take precautions ahead of time since ETAM decisions are final. You will not have the option to appeal your decision.
